About Us

Welcome to VeraVerto, your one-stop destination where you can spend your hard-earned money on getting the best value-for-money deals. We're dedicated to giving you the most elegant yet modern clothing, with a focus on the best style, comfort and affordability. VeraVerto is one of the fastest-growing e-commerce brands in India which offers a unique range of clothing with a shade of western touch. We are the pioneers of bringing western style in the Indian market at very affordable rates.

Founded by three college students Saurabh Raj, Amit Singh and Varikuti Vardhan, VeraVerto has come a long way from its beginnings in a small room in a hostel. When we first started out, our passion for providing the best clothing range drove us to do intense research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over India and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
